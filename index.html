Frequently Ask Questions

  1. Why does the app need to use my location services?

    In the Nearby tab of the Menus4ALL app, we use location services to provide you the closest restaurant menus quickly. You can always update your location preferences by going to the privacy settings on your phone.


  2. Why are only some of the restaurant menus in my area displayed?

    The Menus4ALL app receives menu data from a major provider of restaurant info in the U.S. Currently, we are only able to provide the restaurant menus from those restaurants in the database. Menus4ALL is working to provide even more restaurants in even more cities across the United States.


  3. How can I search for a restaurant?

    Currently, you can use any U.S. zip code to search for a restaurant menu. We are working with our developer to provide additional search functionality in future update.
